MyPharmacist

ONGOING


This system is aimed at developing a prescription management system that will manage patients record and tracking of medication. A doctor will diagnose a patient, patients will get their prescribed medication through delivery and receive notification about the delivery. The patients will also be able to track their medication by keeping count of how many pills they have, how many they take daily and also include if a patient missed pills.
